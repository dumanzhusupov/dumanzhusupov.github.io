Tennis training for children and adults

Tennis for kids in Astana

Tennis King Club is a great place for everyone who loves to play tennis. Our club offers various types of classes, including individual and group trainings, ideal for both beginners and experienced players. To sign up for training, just contact us by phone or send a request via our website. Our professional trainers will offer you an optimal training plan, taking into account your individual needs and level of play. For children, we offer special groups where they can learn the basics of tennis in a fun and safe atmosphere. Our coaches will teach children the basics of the technique of the game, such as forehand and backhand strikes, as well as proper movement on the court. We also offer a game practice that will help children improve their skills and learn how to play as a team.
